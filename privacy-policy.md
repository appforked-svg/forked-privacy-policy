1. Introduction

Welcome to Forked, a restaurant discount and voucher application developed by xcrelease.
This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the Forked mobile app (“the App”).

By using Forked, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect and process the following information:

Account Information: When you sign up or log in, we collect your Google Account or Firebase Authentication data (email address, user ID, profile picture, and optionally your name).

Phone Verification: We may collect your phone number if you verify it for voucher redemption or account security.

Usage Data: Includes app interactions, viewed restaurants, and redeemed vouchers (for analytics and performance).

Device Information: We may collect device type, OS version, and app version for debugging and analytics.

Location Data (Optional): Only if you allow location permissions, we may use it to suggest nearby restaurants.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

Authenticate users securely via Firebase Authentication.

Display restaurant promotions and manage voucher claims.

Improve user experience through analytics (Firebase Analytics).

Communicate essential updates and app features.

Detect and prevent fraud or misuse of vouchers.

4. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored securely in Google Firebase (Firestore, Authentication, and Cloud Functions) hosted in the Asia-Southeast2 region.
We implement industry-standard encryption and access controls to keep your data safe.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information.
We may share limited data only with:

Google Firebase Services (for authentication, cloud storage, and analytics).

Service Providers working under strict confidentiality agreements to maintain the app.

No personal information is shared with restaurant partners beyond aggregated or anonymized usage data.

6. Data Retention

Your account data is retained while your account remains active.
If you delete your account, we will permanently remove or anonymize your personal data within 30 days.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

Access or correct your personal information.

Delete your account or request data removal by contacting us at support@forkedapp.com
.

Revoke permissions such as location access at any time through your device settings.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our app is intended for users aged 18 and above.
We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The updated version will be posted at the same link with a revised effective date.
